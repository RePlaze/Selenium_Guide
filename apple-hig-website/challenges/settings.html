Challenge 1 Design a Settings Page

Challenge Brief

Objective

Create a settings page that follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines. Focus on clear organization, intuitive controls, and visual hierarchy.

Requirements

  • ○ Implement toggle switches for on/off settings
  • ○ Group related settings together
  • ○ Use proper typography hierarchy
  • ○ Apply consistent spacing
  • ○ Make controls interactive and accessible

Design Guidelines

Toggle Switches

Use iOS-style toggle switches with smooth animations and clear on/off states.

Grouping

Separate settings into logical groups with clear section headers.

Descriptions

Add subtle descriptions for complex settings to improve clarity.
